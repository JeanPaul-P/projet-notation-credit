This project is part of my data analysis portfolio. Its primary objective is to analyze and anticipate corporate credit risk using the "Corporate Credit Rating with Financial Ratios" dataset. We hypothesize that this need arises from stakeholders such as credit analysts and risk managers, who must make informed decisions about whether to grant financing. It combines skills in Excel, Python, financial analysis, and data visualization using Power BI.

üîç Project Issues

  • Analyze the factors that influence company credit rating.

  • Create a custom risk score based on financial ratios.
  • Monitor the evolution of ratings and risk over time.
  • Create an intuitive and interactive Power BI dashboard with useful insights for decision-making.
